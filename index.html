Random Catholic Bible Chapter

This tool randomly selects a chapter from the Catholic Bible, which includes the 73 books recognized in the Catholic canon - 46 books of the Old Testament and 27 books of the New Testament.

The Catholic Bible includes 7 deuterocanonical books not found in Protestant Bibles: Tobit, Judith, 1 and 2 Maccabees, Wisdom, Sirach (Ecclesiasticus), and Baruch.
